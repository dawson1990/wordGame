Aim of The Game:


A source word will be presented to you. You need to create 7 words from the source word. The game will begin to time you when you click 'begin' below If successful you will be asked for your name to be entered into leaderboard


RULES:


1. Cannot use source word as an answer

3. Answers cannot be be less than 3 letters long
4. Can have no duplicates
5. Can only use each letter in source word once.
